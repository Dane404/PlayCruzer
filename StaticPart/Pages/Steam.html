History:

Steam started in 1999 with the sucess of Team Fortress Classic and Counter Strike, after that they created the game shop Steam, Steam remained the number one game shop for about 18 years until Epic Games Store released.

Advantages:

Steam has the biggest capacity of games of all the game shops, Steam also has many discounts where almost every game is on sale ,Steam also has the Steam Community where players can publish screenshots, reviews, modifications(mods) for a game etc.

Disadvantages:

The game developers get 70% profit while they get 88% on the Epic Games Store
