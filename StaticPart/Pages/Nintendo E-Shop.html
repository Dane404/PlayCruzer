Nintendo E-Shop

Publisher: Nintendo

Description:

The nintendo e-shop is a store where you can buy games for the Nintendo Switch, 3ds and Wii U. You can either buy products with Nintendo points, Nintendo money cards and other payment methods.

Realese:

6.06.2011

Plattforms:

  • Nintendo 3ds (every model): 6.06.2011
  • Wii U: 18.11.2012
  • Nintendo Switch: 3.3.2017

History:

The Nintendo e-shop was first published on the 6 June 2011 on the handheld console named Nintendo 3ds. Each console generation the store got faster and faster( on the 3ds the store was very slow but on the switch is isn´t that slow anymore)

Advantages:

  • Many exclusive titles
  • On the 3ds or the wii u you can buy older games from other consoles (virtual console etc.)

Disadvantages:

  • The most game titles cost the same as if it was released
  • At the Nintendo Switch version of this shop as an example the virtual console games aren’t available.
