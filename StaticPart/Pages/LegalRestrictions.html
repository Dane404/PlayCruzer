Legal Restrictions

When is it legal or illegal to download games?

If the game is copyrighted, it is illegal to download from any piracy site. However, if the game is under creative commons or public domain, it is legal to download from any piracy site.

How does the USK check Games?

Like films, all games sold on the German market are evaluated according to criteria intended to protect minors; these games are assigned a fitting age rating by the Entertainment Software Self-Regulation Body (USK)

How do you check if a games shop is legit?
