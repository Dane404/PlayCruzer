Epic Games Store

Publisher: Epic Games

Description:

Epic Gams is a game shop, which management is situated in Raleigh in South Carolina. It is one of the most used game shops on the entire world. Epic Games Store has over 61 million active monthly users.

Content:

Games for Windows, Linux, MacOS and ChromeOS

Plattforms:

PC

Realese:

Epic Games Store was founded on 6th. December 2018 for Windows.

History and Evolution:

A short time after the release of Fortnite, on the 6. December 2018 Epic Games Store was founded to counter steam on the global market.

Advantages:

They are having a lot of discounts. One time a month, they offer one game for free. Developer receive 88% of the profit of the game, while they receive only 70% on steam.

Disadvantages:

Epic Games Store exists since 2018, so they are not having the capacity of game shops like steam.
