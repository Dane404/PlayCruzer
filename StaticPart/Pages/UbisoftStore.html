Ubisoft-Connect

Publisher: Ubisoft

Description:

Ubisoft-Connect previously U-Play is used exclusively by Ubisoft games althoug they sell some other games too.

Realese:

U-Play was first published for Pc in 2009 while the Ubisoft-Connect rebranding was in 2020.

History:

U-Play started with the sucess of the Assassins Creed series(in 2009) after that they introduced the Ubisoft Club (in 2015) and in 2020 they retired the Ubisoft Club and the U-Play Store in favor for a new service Ubisoft-Connect

Advantages:

Ubisoft-Connect has Ubisoft Plus where you can pay some money per month to have acess to every game of Ubisoft as well as for upcoming beta tests.

Disadvantages:

They have a low capacity of games compared to other game shops because almost every game they sell is from Ubisoft.
